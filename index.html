Alice Quinn

Actress Olivia Taylor-Dudley as Alice Quinn, from the SyFy TV show The Magicians.


Early Life

Alice was born in 1993 in Chicago, Illinois to a family of magicians; parents, Daniel and Stephanie Quinn, and older brother Charlie Quinn.

Charlie attended Brakebills University for Magical Pedagogy until his mysterious death in 2014.

Despite her talent and powerful magical abilities, Alice was not invited to Brakebills, and instead found the University by herself and was allowed to take the Entrance Exam.

Once enrolled at Brakebills, Alice began investigating her brother's death, eventually discovering that he had transformed into a Niffin, a being of pure magic

Magical Abilities

Alice's Discipline is Phosphoromancy, a physical branch of magic that enables her to bend light.

Significant Relationships

Alice was especially close with her brother Charlie, who would sing the Simple Minds song 'Don't You Forget About Me' to cheer her up.

In 2016 Alice has a short-lived romantic relationship with Quentin Coldwater, which ends after he cheats on her with Margo Hanson and Eliot Waugh.
